Submission Guideline

Salix Review is published 2 times a year in June and December. The reading period
for each issue is as follows: March, April and May for the June issue and September,
October and November for the December issue.
Simultaneous Salix Review considers unpublished poems,short fiction and art. An unpublished
poem/fiction/art is one that has not appeared in any form of print or digital media,
including personal or public blogs.
Simultaneous submissions are fine but please do inform us as soon as your work is
accepted elsewhere. Hope your honest in this aspect. We read/evaluate every
submission with great passion and sensibility and respond to each submission.
Before submitting, please read your work several times and make it flawless and of
great standard. We don’t see your previous publication history, your degrees,
geographical background and other credentials. At Salix Review incredible quality
is the only criterion for acceptance.
no more than 5 poems each limited
to 400 words, 1 short fiction limited to 2000 words and 5 pieces of arts per reading period with a fee of USD $ 3.
If accepted, Salix Review claims First Electronic Rights,
and First Indian Rights, reflecting that publications here at Salix Review must be the first publication to
publish the work online and/or in print.
Salix Review welcomes, receives donations if someone voluntarily comes forward to
donate for the cause as the website is managed by freelancers and we pay them for
their work. At last, we regret to say that Salix Review is unable to pay to its authors
and artists for their contributions but things will certainly change in the future.
