Afresh Frankincense (12) Founder and Art Editor

Afresh Frankincense is 12-year-old and in Class 7th. He’s a child art-prodigy and writer from Odisha and lives in Hyderabad in India.  Though he loves math and science so much, art has a special place in his heart. His work appears or forthcoming in Blue Marble Review, The Elephant Ladder, Moonchild Magazine, The Celestal Review, The Ekphrastic Review, Kids 4G and elsewhere.

Pitambar Naik, Editor

Pitambar Naik is an advertising conceptualiser for a living. He’s been featured in journals across 13 countries. He’s a Poetry Editor for Minute Magazine. His recent work appears or forthcoming in Another Chicago Magazine, Packingtown Review, Nether, Mason Street Review, Rigorous, New Contrast, Ghost City Review, Glass Poetry, Cha Journal, The Indian Quarterly, and The World Belongs To Us HarperCollins India among others. The Anatomy of Solitude (Hawakal) is his debut book of poetry. He grew up in Odisha, India.
